MAVProxy

A UAV ground station software package for MAVLink based systems

Calibration

There are several commands to calibrate the various sensors on the APM:

accelcal

Initiates the accelerometer calibration routines. This is the "place the UAV on it's left side..." routine.

calpress

Initiates the pressure sensor calibration routines.

ground

Performs a full ground start. This includes gyro and pressure sensor calibration. It is automatically performed when the APM switched to armed.
